Näive humanism in Key the Metal Idol

ID: 2
Creation: 2023-05-08
Last modification: 2023-05-17

Tags: #incomplete, #proposition, #reconstruction of psychoanalysis

Key the Metal Idol has a peculiar plot twist during its last two episodes: Key is not a robot, but a human forced to have her humanity repressed in order to be protected from exploitation. When compared to works like Serial Experiments Lain (Anime) or Ghost in the Shell (1995 film), Key the Metal Idol is far more humanist: not a single instance of AI appears throughout the OVA, and most conflicts during the plot center around robots (and technology in general) being used by humans to exploit other humans and (quite literally) steal their life.
